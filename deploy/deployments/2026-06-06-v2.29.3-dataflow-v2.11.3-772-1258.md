## kailash-dataflow 2.11.3 — type-introspection consolidation (#772)

Consolidates two-spelling Optional/Union detection (`typing.Union`/`Optional` AND PEP 604 `X | None`) into the single primitive `dataflow.core.type_introspection.union_non_none_args()`, plus `strip_annotated()` for `Annotated[T] → T` normalization. Routed **10 caller-methods** through the primitive (each keeps its own post-detection policy): `type_processor` (`_resolve_type`, `validate_field`), `nodes` (`_normalize_type_annotation`, `_normalize_id_type`, `_unwrap_optional_type`), `engine` (`_python_type_to_sql_type`), `schema` (`_parse_field`), `model_validator` (×2), `fk_aware` (`_is_nullable_field`), `model_registry` (`_normalize_field_type`).

Root cause closed: #1228 (PEP 604) had to patch the union-spelling check at every site independently — the maintenance tax #772 predicted. **Behavior-preserving** (the only behavior delta is the latent `Annotated`-alias-leak fix: `_resolve_type(Annotated[int])` now returns `int` instead of the bare `Annotated` alias).

PR #1270 (merge `f97e6bbbb`, branch `refactor/772-consolidate-type-introspection`, signed `f15db7206`). Release-prep PR #1271 (`release/dataflow-v2.11.3`, merge `5a3653e29`).
